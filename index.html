Famous Nepali Dishes

Dal Bhat Momo Sel Roti

Dal Bhat

Dal Bhat is the national dish of Nepal. It consists of steamed rice served with lentil soup, vegetables, and sometimes meat curry. It is a staple meal providing energy and nutrition to Nepali people.

Momo

Momo are Nepali dumplings filled with minced meat or vegetables, often served with a spicy dipping sauce. They are one of the most popular street foods in Nepal.

Sel Roti

Sel Roti is a traditional homemade, sweet, ring-shaped rice bread/doughnut. It is especially prepared during Nepali festivals like Dashain and Tihar, enjoyed with yogurt or curry.
